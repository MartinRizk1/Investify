Technical Indicators LIVE

Relative Strength Index (RSI)

RSI measures momentum on a scale of 0 to 100. Values over 70 indicate overbought conditions (possible reversal down), while values under 30 suggest oversold conditions (possible reversal up).

Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)

MACD shows the relationship between two moving averages. When MACD crosses above the signal line, it's a bullish sign. When it crosses below, it's bearish.

Bollinger Bands

Bollinger Bands show volatility channels around a moving average. Price reaching the upper band may indicate overbought conditions, while touching the lower band may signal oversold conditions.
